Surveys

Each seminar will have one or more surveys depending on the time of the seminar. Surveys are created to assess the quality of teaching, collect information from students and Staff after field trips

About Educenter

The Education was born with the noble mission of conveying the spirit of environmental protection to the younger generations, who will become the pillars of the future. Since its establishment in 2020, The Education, with 3 campuses from North to South, has organized hundreds of seminars at universities, colleges, large and small centers. With a teaching staff of up to 50 people, the seminars are professionally designed with rich content, attracting young people. The Education is confident to be the Top 1 institution in organizing environmental seminars.
